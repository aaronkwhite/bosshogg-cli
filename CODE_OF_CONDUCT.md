# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, welcoming, and productive experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, identity, or affiliation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints, experiences, and technical opinions.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Show empathy toward other contributors.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Public or private harassment of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

The maintainer is responsible for clarifying these standards and may take corrective action — including edits to comments, issue or PR closure, or contributor bans — in response to behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

## Reporting

Report violations to **aaron@aaronkwhite.com**. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion. The maintainer is obligated to respect the privacy and safety of reporters.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (repository, issues, pull requests, discussions) and when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.
