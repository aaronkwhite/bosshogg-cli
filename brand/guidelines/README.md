## Quick reference

**Name:** BossHogg (two G's; never "Boss Hog", "BossHog", or "bosshog")
**Descriptor:** The agent-first PostHog CLI
**Tagline:** PostHog power, right in your prompt.

**Colors:**
- Orange: `#FF6A00` (primary brand; "Hogg" in wordmark)
- Orange-light: `#FF8C33` (secondary accent)
- Cream: `#F2EBD5` (warm background)
- Black: `#0D0D0D`
- Charcoal: `#1A1A1A`
- Green: `#22C55E` (success/prompt state)

**Logo:** Horizontal wordmark — **Boss** in black/white, **Hogg** in `#FF6A00`, Inter ExtraBold, -0.04em letter-spacing.
